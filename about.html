Craft process

From sketch to polished finish.

Each collection begins with a sketch, becomes a carefully balanced form, and is finished with a presentation that feels gift-ready and refined.

1. Sketch

Shapes are explored on paper until the proportions feel balanced and elegant.

2. Refine

Materials and finishes are selected to create a cohesive look across the collection.

3. Present

The final piece is packaged and prepared to feel special from the first moment.
