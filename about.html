Our story

Built for curious people who love exploring cities.

City Trails is a small guide site focused on simple, memorable experiences you can enjoy in a few hours, without heavy planning.

Mission Help people find places that feel local, relaxed, and worth revisiting.
Approach Short recommendations, clear details, and practical tips for real visits.
What you will find Curated suggestions for cafes, quiet streets, and neighborhood favorites.
What makes it useful Easy-to-scan content, honest notes, and ideas that fit into busy schedules.

How we curate

Simple picks, practical details, better weekends.

Each recommendation is selected for atmosphere, accessibility, and value so you can decide quickly and enjoy more of your time out.

1. Discover

Explore neighborhoods and note standout places with local character.

2. Validate

Check opening hours, pricing, and practical details for visitors.

3. Share

Publish concise guides that are easy to read on desktop and mobile.
